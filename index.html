The Story of our inception!

How we evolved?

TenSketch is revolutionary IT start-up founded on 28th August 2017 by a group of young and dynamic professionals from various fields. They had a burning desire to take up Entrepreneurship as a major tool to solve challenges of society and business world. The founders carries with them a diverse range of skillset in the field of IT, Management, Marketing, Human Resources, etc. They Started the organization with a major goal to provide Holistic web design solutions to all levels of business and society and has also planned to expand the other verticals of software and technology.

Our Uniqueness

Technical Consulting and Creative Consulting. We make a thorough analysis of our client’s requirement and make a study of their field. We take the design process only after analysis stage. We present a Business Analysis document to the client which serves as creative consulting for their business which specifies the scope of improvement in contents. After the client accepts the changes, we proceed with the design and development.
